Bata Yaki Sauce Recipe

Dean Taise

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup shoyu
  • 1 Cup brown sugar
  • 1 Cup water
  • 1 package dashinomoto
  • 2 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp ginger juice
  • 3 tsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp ajinomoto(optional)
  • 1 tsp seasame seeds, crushed
  • 1 tsp tabasco sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame seed oil
  • green onions, chopped

    • Steps

  • boil shoyu, brown sugar, water, dashinomoto
  • stir until sugar has disolved and remove from heat and set aside to cool
  • add rest of ingrediants to cooled sauce
  • stir together and serve in bowls for dipping for each person
  • Serve with your choice of foods
