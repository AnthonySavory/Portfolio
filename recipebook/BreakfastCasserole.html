Breakfast Casserole Recipe

Savory Family

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup of half and half
  • 2 tbsp of green onions (chopped)
  • salt and pepper
  • 6 slices of texas toast/french toast bread(thick cut white bread)
  • 1 lb of spicy pork sausage(cooked and drained of fat)
  • 1 cup of grated cheddar chesse
  • butter to grease 12x10 inch baking pan

    • Steps

  • In small bowl, beat eggs until they loosen
  • Add half and half and green onions. Mix well, highly season with salt and pepper
  • Butter a 12x10 inch baking pan
  • Line pan with bread,cutting and rearranging,if needed.
  • S[rinkle cooked sausage and cheese over th bread
  • pour egg mixture over the entire pan
  • cover and refrigerate overnight
  • preheat oven to 350 degrees fehrenheit
  • cook covered for 25-30 minutes or until egg mixture is set
