Pancake Recipe

Joshua Weissman

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1½ cups of milk
  • 2 cups of ap flour
  • ¼ cups of granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp of sea salt
  • ¼ tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • 2 tbsp of unsalted butter

    • Steps

  • Put egg and milk into bowl (wet)
  • Whisk together
  • Put flour, sugar, salt, baking soda, and baking powder into a seperate bowl (dry)
  • Whisk together
  • Combine the dry with the wet
  • melt butter
  • while stirring add butter to wet and dry mix
  • Let rest for 5 mins.
  • Use ¼ cup to portion servings
  • Cook on medium heat
